I don't know about you, but a lot of people think Berkeley elevators can be some of the sketchiest elevators out there.
Quachy was one day making his way through a Berkeley apartment complex, all ready for his date. He opened the door and stepped into the elevator.
The elevator broke.
Quachy is stuck.

Moral of the story. Don't be like Quachy, keep your people happy despite the broken elevator.
