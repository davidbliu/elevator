Problem Description

You are a rich business dude/diva and have too much money to spend so you decide to build elevators! You've created a new, simple kind of elevator that only does three things: idle, move, and switch.

Terminology

  • Request: When customers want to be delivered from floor A to floor B they make a request for an elevator. Their request consists of 4 important pieces of information: name, floor A, floor B, and time (the time they requested to be delivered from their start floor to their end floor)
  • Elevators
    1. Idle
    2. Move
    3. Switch
